# Hackathon #001 — Fever Bot

> Auto-syncing Fever calendar feed and pregame iMessages for one very devoted superfan.

**Status:** in flight
**Started:** 2026-05-27T03:58:54Z
**Time budget:** 60 min
**Tags:** WNBA, iMessage, GoogleCalendar

## Problem

Mitch's wife is a Caitlin Clark and Indiana Fever superfan. Staying on top of every game means juggling ESPN, Yahoo Sports, the team site, and her own calendar — apps that are too cluttered for someone who isn't techy. She just wants the right info to land in front of her at the right time, on the device she already uses.

## Definition of done

A subscribable Google Calendar feed covering every remaining 2026 Indiana Fever game with opponent, broadcast channel, and tipoff time in her local zone, **plus** a scheduled iMessage that fires 15 minutes before each tipoff with verified starting lineup and one or two pregame news beats — in sports-radio voice. Wife uses zero Claude tooling.
